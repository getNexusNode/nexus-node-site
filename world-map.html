What it does

Four ways to use the map

Every contact with an address gets pinned automatically. Then the map becomes a tool.

Auto-Geocoding

Every contact with a saved address gets pinned on the map automatically. No manual coordinates needed — NEXUS NODE handles it in the background.

Home Base

Mark your current home location. The map automatically shows distances from your base to each contact, making it easy to see who's closeby.

Travel Mode

Heading somewhere? Drop a travel pin anywhere in the world. NEXUS NODE instantly shows you all contacts near that location so you can plan meetups.

Wishlist Places

Save places you want to visit someday — cities, countries, venues. Your wishlist pins show up on the map alongside your contacts for the full picture.
