Network Graph

Your relationships,
made visible.

The graph builds itself. Every contact is a node — connections form based on shared categories, relationships and more.

Force-Directed Layout

Nodes repel and attract based on connection strength. Tight clusters of close contacts naturally pull together — loose acquaintances stay at the edges.

Influence Score

NEXUS NODE calculates how influential each person is in your network — based on connections, warmth, and centrality. Bigger node = higher influence.

Shortest Path

Pick any two people in your network. NEXUS NODE highlights the shortest connection path between them — useful for warm introductions.

Color Clusters

Contacts in the same category automatically cluster together with a shared color. See your Work, Family and Friends groups form right before your eyes.

Interactive Graph

Drag, zoom,
explore.

The graph is fully interactive. Drag any node to reposition it. Zoom in on dense clusters. Click a node to highlight its direct connections and see the person's card.

  • Drag nodes freely to rearrange
  • Scroll to zoom in and out
  • Click node → highlight all direct edges
  • Filter graph by category in one click
