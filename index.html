FAQ

Common questions

Does it work offline? Is there a cloud?
100% offline. NEXUS NODE stores all your data locally on your device in %APPDATA%\NexusNode. No account needed, no internet required after activation. Your data never leaves your machine.
What happens after I buy? How do I activate?
After purchase on Gumroad you receive a license key by email. Open NEXUS NODE → Settings → License → paste your key → Done! PRO is activated instantly, works forever on that device.
Can I import my existing contacts?
Yes! PRO users can import from Google Contacts via vCard (.vcf) export. Go to Google Contacts → Export → vCard → import into NEXUS NODE. All names, emails, phones and addresses are imported automatically.
Is Mac supported?
Currently Windows 10/11 only. Mac support is planned for v1.2 — the codebase is already prepared for it. Sign up to the newsletter or follow updates on Gumroad to be notified.
Can I get a refund?
